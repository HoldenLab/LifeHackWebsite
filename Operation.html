A circuit diagram style drawing of control connections as layed out on our iteration of LifeHack.

TriggerScope

  • DAC1: 405nm laser power control
  • DAC2: 488nm laser power control
  • DAC3: AOTF (560nm channel)
  • DAC4: AOTF (642nm channel)
  • TTL-Out1: 405nm laser triggering
  • TTL-Out2: 488nm laser triggering
  • TTL-Out3: Shutter triggering
  • TTL-Out4: Camera triggering
  • TTL-In1: Camera triggering

The AOTF signal generator takes input from the TriggerScope via a 25 pin connector. Therefore a custom connector is required to convert from SMA. This just requires a blank 25 pin connector and 3 SMA-Blank wires to solder into the required pins. Care must be taken to reinforce the finished assembly as otherwise it is extremely fragile.
